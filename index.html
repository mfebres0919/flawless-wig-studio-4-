Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked Questions

How long does a wig install take? +

Most installs take between 1.5 to 3 hours depending on the style and technique. Lace melts and detailed baby hair work may take a bit longer. You'll receive a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to bring my own wig? +

Yes! We install your wig — we don't supply one. Please arrive with a clean, pre-plucked, and pre-bleached wig ready to go. If you need help sourcing quality hair, we're happy to point you in the right direction.

What's the difference between a frontal and closure install? +

A frontal runs ear to ear across the top of your head, giving more styling versatility including pulled-back looks. A closure is smaller (usually 4x4 or 5x5) and sits in the middle — great for natural-looking parts without a full frontal.

How long will my install last? +

With proper care, an install typically lasts 2–4 weeks. We recommend sleeping with a satin bonnet, avoiding excess moisture at the roots, and coming in for a maintenance touch-up as needed.

Is a deposit required to book? +

Yes — a $25 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This deposit goes toward your total. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid forfeiting your deposit.

Can I get my wig styled on the same day as my install? +

Absolutely! Just let us know when booking so we can schedule enough time. We do cuts, curls, flat iron, and more — just bring your inspiration photos!

What if I need to reschedule? +

Life happens! You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Use the link in your confirmation email or contact us directly and we'll find a new time that works for you.
